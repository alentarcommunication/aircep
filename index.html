COMMON QUESTIONS

Simple answers before you begin

Does the AI replace my staff?

No. It supports staff by handling routine, overflow and after-hours conversations, while complex or sensitive matters can be routed to people.

Can I start with only missed calls or after-hours calls?

Yes. A focused starting workflow is often the simplest way to test and refine the experience.

Can it capture leads and booking requests?

Yes. It can collect structured details and support appointment or service requests based on your configured workflow and integrations.
